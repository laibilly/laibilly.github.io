‘The Love of Couture’ x ARTE M Hong Kong - A Journey to the Legacy of Fashion and Digital Media

Kick off your holiday season of art and culture with two unprecedented, talk-of-the-town exhibitions in Hong Kong!

‘The Love of Couture: Artisanship in Fashion Beyond Time’ is Asia’s first exhibition celebrating 200 years of couture, showcasing a cultural fashion exchange from east to west, past to present and future. Developed by K11 in collaboration with the V&A, the world’s leading museum in London, and designed by internationally acclaimed Production Designer William Chang Suk Ping with 6 emerging East Asian designers, ‘The Love of Couture’ promises to amaze with pieces dating back to the 1830s along with grand contemporary responses that highlight the importance of fashion through history.

ARTE M Hong Kong, this season’s most highly anticipated digital art presentation, takes you on a multisensory virtual journey under the theme “Eternal Nature”. Powered by cutting-edge technology from South Korea, the vivid Instagram hotspot is the place to experience the latest frontier of art.   Get your two-in-one ticket to visit ‘The Love of Couture’ and ARTE M Hong Kong now!
